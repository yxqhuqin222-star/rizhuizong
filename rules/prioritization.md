## House rules (flag, never gate)

These rules guide what the system surfaces. They do not block work. The PM decides.

- **5 risk areas check.** When a feature is selected for delivery, flag any of the 5 risk areas (value, usability, feasibility, viability, **other**) that don't yet have at least one hypothesis. Do not block delivery — surface the gap so the PM can decide whether to write a hypothesis, accept the risk, or ignore it.
- **Strategy conflict surfacing.** When a new bet, feature, opportunity, or user need conflicts with `knowledge/strategy.md`, surface the conflict — don't reject. New signals can inform strategy just as much as strategy informs them. The PM resolves by updating strategy, rejecting the signal, or holding both as a noted tension.
